Skill: Differentiate Polynomials

Goal: Differentiate quickly and accurately using the power rule.

Method

  1. For \(ax^n\), bring down \(n\) and reduce power by 1.
  2. Constants differentiate to 0.

Worked Example

If \(y = 3x^4 - 5x^2 + 7x - 9\), then

\(\dfrac{dy}{dx} = 12x^3 - 10x + 7\)

Common mistake: forgetting to reduce the power.

Quick Practice

  1. \(y = 6x^5 + 2x^3 - x\)
  2. \(y = 4x^{-2} + 9x\)
  3. \(y = (3x^2 + 1)(x-4)\) (expand first)
